Norway Bank Digital Attendance & Workforce Tracking Platform

Banking & Financial Services • Europe • Confidential Engagement

Project Overview

Client: Norway Bank (Confidential)
Project Code: LTI-NB-BFSI-2025
Industry: BFSI
Geography: Europe (GDPR)
Engagement: Enterprise Managed Services
Status: Active – Development & Integration

The Norway Bank Digital Attendance & Workforce Tracking Platform is a secure, cloud-native enterprise solution built to modernize workforce governance across large-scale banking operations.

The platform leverages AI-driven validation, geo-fencing, and compliance-first architecture to replace legacy attendance mechanisms while aligning with European BFSI regulatory mandates.

Key Functional Capabilities

  • Location-verified attendance with configurable geo-fencing
  • AI-based anomaly & proxy attendance detection
  • RBAC for Employees, Managers, HR & Admins
  • Real-time dashboards and productivity analytics
  • Automated BFSI-aligned audit reporting
  • Secure approval workflows for remote work

Technology Stack

Backend: Java (Spring Boot), Microservices
AI Layer: Python, ML Pipelines, Risk Models
Streaming: Apache Kafka
Databases: Oracle, PostgreSQL
Security: OAuth 2.0, JWT, RBAC, Encryption
Cloud: AWS / Azure Hybrid
DevOps: Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD
Compliance: GDPR, ISO 27001, SOC 2

Business Impact

  • Significant reduction in attendance fraud
  • Improved workforce transparency
  • Audit-ready compliance posture
  • AI-driven operational insights
  • Future-ready HR automation foundation
