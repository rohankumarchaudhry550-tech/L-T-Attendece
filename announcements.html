📢 Darbhanga Branch Operational Announcement

🏢 Official Update

We are delighted to officially announce that our Darbhanga branch will become fully operational from 15th April. This expansion marks an important step in strengthening our presence and providing better opportunities for our employees.

Employees who are interested in joining, relocating, or transferring to the Darbhanga branch are invited to express their interest by contacting their respective HR department. All requests will be reviewed and processed based on availability, role requirements, and internal approval guidelines.

To ensure a smooth transition and comfortable working experience, the company will provide the following facilities to selected employees:

  • 🏠 PG / Accommodation Facility
  • 🍽️ Food & Meal Support
  • 🌐 High-Speed Internet Access
  • 🛠️ Basic Living & Workplace Essentials

The Darbhanga branch is designed to offer a productive, supportive, and growth-oriented work environment. This is an excellent opportunity for employees looking to explore new responsibilities, gain experience, and work in a well-supported setup.

Kindly note that allocation to this branch will be subject to internal approvals and operational requirements. Early communication with HR is highly recommended for smooth processing.

For further information, detailed guidelines, or to initiate your request, please reach out to HR through official communication channels. Additional updates will be shared as required.

🏆 Inter DC Championship 2026 — Official Announcement

🚫 Registration Closed

We are excited to announce that the Inter DC Championship 2026 will officially commence from 1st April. This event aims to encourage team spirit, discipline, and active participation across all departments.

Please note that the registration window is now closed, and no further entries or modifications will be accepted under any circumstances.

🎯 Eligibility & Participation Guidelines

  • Participation is mandatory for all registered employees
  • Eligible age group: Below 35 years
  • Each participant can compete in a maximum of 3–4 events
  • A minimum of 2 hours of daily practice is compulsory

📋 Performance & Compliance Requirements

  • Daily practice video submission is mandatory for all participants
  • Videos must be uploaded through the official system within the given timeframe
  • Consistency in practice and reporting will be monitored by the management

⚠️ Important Instructions

  • No new or late registrations will be accepted
  • Failure to comply with practice or submission rules may lead to disciplinary action
  • Participants must strictly follow all guidelines and schedules
  • Final decisions will be taken by DC Management and will be binding

Ensure your daily practice is recorded and submitted on time.

For any queries or clarifications, please contact your respective coordinators or HR team.
