Norway Bank Digital Attendance & Workforce Tracking Platform

Banking & Financial Services | Europe | Confidential Program

Project Overview

Client: Norway Bank (Confidential)
Project Code: LTI-NB-BFSI-2025
Industry: Banking & Financial Services (BFSI)
Geography: Europe (GDPR Regulated)
Engagement Model: Enterprise Managed Services
Status: Active – Development & Integration Phase

The Norway Bank Digital Attendance & Workforce Tracking Platform is a secure, cloud-native, enterprise solution designed to modernize workforce management for large-scale banking operations. The platform replaces legacy attendance systems with an AI-enabled, location-aware, and compliance-driven architecture aligned with European BFSI regulatory standards.

The solution enables real-time employee attendance capture using geo-fencing, GPS validation, and device fingerprinting while ensuring strict adherence to GDPR, data residency, and information security policies. The platform is engineered to support high concurrency, multi-region deployments, and seamless integration with existing banking IT ecosystems.

Key Functional Capabilities

  • Location-verified digital attendance with configurable geo-fencing radius
  • AI-based anomaly detection for proxy attendance and suspicious behavior
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) for Employees, Managers, HR, and Admins
  • Real-time workforce dashboards and productivity analytics
  • Automated compliance reporting aligned with BFSI audit requirements
  • Secure approval workflows for remote work and location exceptions

Technology Stack

Core Banking Platform: Java (Spring Boot), AI-enabled Microservices Architecture
AI & Intelligence Layer: Python, Machine Learning Pipelines, Anomaly Detection & Predictive Risk Models
Decision & Risk Engine: AI-driven Rule Engine, Real-time Fraud Detection & Alerting
Integration Layer: REST & SOAP Services, Enterprise Service Bus (ESB)
Data Management: Oracle Database, PostgreSQL (High-availability & ACID-compliant)
Streaming & Event Processing: Apache Kafka for Real-time Transaction Analysis
Security Framework: OAuth 2.0, JWT, RBAC, Encryption at Rest & In-Transit
Identity & Access Management: LDAP / Active Directory with Policy-based Controls
Cloud & Infrastructure: AWS / Azure Hybrid Cloud, Containerized Workloads
DevOps & Observability: CI/CD Pipelines, Docker, Kubernetes, Centralized Logging & Monitoring
Compliance & Governance: ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, BFSI Regulatory Standards

Architecture & Design Principles

The system follows a microservices-based architecture ensuring scalability, fault isolation, and independent service deployments. Each module is designed with security-by-design principles, incorporating centralized logging, monitoring, and audit trails.

The platform integrates seamlessly with existing HRMS, payroll, and identity management systems through secure APIs, enabling minimal disruption to existing banking workflows.

Business Impact

  • Reduction in attendance fraud and manual intervention
  • Improved workforce transparency and operational efficiency
  • Enhanced compliance posture for regulatory audits
  • Actionable insights through AI-driven workforce analytics
  • Future-ready foundation for advanced HR automation
