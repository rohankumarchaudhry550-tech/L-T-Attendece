Work Location Status — Valid Till End of March

Official Notice

This is to formally inform all employees that there will be no change in work location for any employee until the end of March.

Employees currently working under Work From Home (WFH) arrangements must continue working from their approved and registered work location.

Employees assigned to Work From Office (WFO) are required to continue reporting to their designated office as per existing company policies and instructions.

Additional Facilities for WFH Employees:

  • Rent Reimbursement: Effective from 1st March, eligible employees may claim rent reimbursement as per company policy. Valid monthly rent payment proof must be submitted to HR for verification and processing.
  • Daily Food Order Facility: Starting 7th March, employees will be eligible to place daily food orders through their official company email ID with a maximum limit of ₹300 per day. Any amount exceeding this limit will be borne by the employee.
  • Food Delivery Availability Notice: In certain locations where food delivery platforms such as Zomato or Swiggy have limited or no service availability, employees may face difficulty placing food orders through these platforms.

    Some of the locations currently experiencing such limitations include Hajipur, Samastipur, Muzaffarpur outskirts, Begusarai rural areas, Darbhanga outskirts, and nearby semi-urban regions of Bihar.

    In such cases, employees may arrange meals locally. Employees are requested to submit their grocery bills or average monthly food expense details to the HR department. HR will review and process the request, and the approved reimbursement amount will be credited during the end-of-month payment cycle.
  • Internet Bill Reimbursement: Effective from 1st March, employees may submit their monthly internet bills required for official work. Valid bill copies must be provided to HR for approval and reimbursement processing.
  • Approval & Compliance: All reimbursements will be processed subject to verification, approval, and compliance with company financial policies.
  • Review Clause: The above facilities are subject to periodic review by management and may be revised depending on operational requirements.

These facilities are applicable only to employees working under WFH arrangements and are subject to internal company policies and approvals.

Any deviation from the approved work location without prior approval will be treated as a compliance violation and may lead to disciplinary action.

Salary Credit Update — Status Notification

Official Update

This is to formally inform all employees that the salary disbursement process is currently in progress.

Salary credits for employees associated during the period 2016 to 2025 are being processed in phases. A significant portion has already been successfully credited, while approximately 2360 records from the 2025 batch till April are still pending and under review.

The remaining salary payments are currently under verification and manual processing. Credits are being executed sequentially according to the company's internal payroll and financial protocols.

The final timeline for salary credit may vary depending on employee record verification, date of joining, and internal data alignment.

Employees are kindly requested to remain patient while the processing cycle is completed. Please note that individual prioritization requests or escalations cannot be accommodated during this phase.

Further updates will be communicated through official company channels once the remaining salary credits are completed.
