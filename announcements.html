🏆 Inter DC Championship 2026 — 7-Phase Sports Timeline

📅 7 Phase Schedule

The Inter DC Championship is now restructured as a 7-Phase tournament with a complete timeline of indoor and outdoor sports. All sports are included with team and individual categories to maximize participation and engagement.

🏃‍♀️ Full Sports List (Indoor + Outdoor)

  • Outdoor: Football, Cricket, Volleyball, Athletics (100m, 400m, Relay), Basketball, Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, Tennis
  • Indoor: Badminton, Table Tennis, Chess, Carrom, Weightlifting, Indoor Handball, Netball

🕒 7-Phase Event Timeline

  1. Phase 1: Opening ceremony, athletics prelims, table tennis pool matches
  2. Phase 2: Football group stage, badminton singles round 1, chess rapid
  3. Phase 3: Basketball quarterfinals, cricket toss + group matches, carrom event
  4. Phase 4: Volleyball prelims, tennis singles, kho-kho and kabaddi pools
  5. Phase 5: Athletics semifinals, badminton doubles, weightlifting
  6. Phase 6: Cricket semifinals, volleyball semifinals, indoor handball
  7. Phase 7: Finals: Cricket, Volleyball, Badminton + closing ceremony and prize distribution

⚠️ Event Guidance

  • All participants must register with their department coordinator 24 hours before the start.
  • Anyone participating in outdoor sports should carry proper shoes and hydration.
  • Indoor players should follow venue safety and equipment rules.
  • Score results will be updated daily on notice board and internal portal.

Key Highlight: Final star events are Cricket, Volleyball, and Badminton. These events are scheduled in the last two days with maximum audience support.

For questions about team allocation, scheduling, or transport, please contact your sports coordinator or HR team.
