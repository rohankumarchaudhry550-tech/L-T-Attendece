🏆 L and T Infotech Inter DC Championship 2026
Complete Championship Schedule & Events
⚽ Phase 1: Soccer Championship (April 1–3)
April 1: League Stage Day 1
  • Opening Ceremony: 10:00 AM - Welcome speech, team introductions, and flag hoisting.
  • Format: Round-robin group stage with 4 teams per group.
  • Rules: 90 minutes match time, standard FIFA rules, 3 points for win, 1 for draw.
  • Matches: Group A: Team 1 vs Team 2 (11:00 AM), Team 3 vs Team 4 (2:00 PM); Group B: Team 5 vs Team 6 (4:00 PM).
  • Team Registration: Verification of players and equipment.
April 2: League Stage Day 2
  • Matches: Group A: Team 1 vs Team 3 (10:00 AM), Team 2 vs Team 4 (12:00 PM); Group B: Team 5 vs Team 7 (2:00 PM), Team 6 vs Team 8 (4:00 PM).
  • Fitness Session: 5:00 PM - Referee training and player fitness checks.
  • Qualification: Top 2 from each group advance to knockout.
April 3: Knockout Stage
  • Semifinals: Group A Winner vs Group B Runner-up (10:00 AM), Group B Winner vs Group A Runner-up (12:00 PM).
  • Final: Winners of semifinals (3:00 PM).
  • Announcement: Top teams qualify for inter-college championship.
  • Rules: Extra time and penalties if needed.
🏀 Phase 2: Basketball & Tennis Championship (April 9–11)
April 9: Basketball Quarterfinals & Tennis Round 1
  • Basketball Format: Single-elimination quarterfinals, 4 x 10-minute quarters.
  • Basketball Rules: FIBA rules, 5 players per team, 24-second shot clock.
  • Basketball Matches: Quarterfinal 1 (9:00 AM), Quarterfinal 2 (11:00 AM).
  • Tennis Format: Round-robin singles matches.
  • Tennis Rules: Best of 3 sets, 6 games per set, tiebreak at 6-6.
  • Tennis Matches: Match 1 (1:00 PM), Match 2 (3:00 PM).
April 10: Basketball Quarterfinals & Tennis Round 2
  • Basketball Matches: Quarterfinal 3 (9:00 AM), Quarterfinal 4 (11:00 AM).
  • Tennis Matches: Match 3 (1:00 PM), Match 4 (3:00 PM).
  • Scoring: Basketball - points for baskets; Tennis - games and sets.
  • Qualification: Winners advance to next round.
April 11: Basketball Semifinals & Tennis Quarterfinals
  • Basketball Semifinals: Winner QF1 vs Winner QF4 (9:00 AM), Winner QF2 vs Winner QF3 (11:00 AM).
  • Tennis Quarterfinals: Top players from round 2 (1:00 PM onwards).
  • Explanation: Intense matches with crowd support and live commentary.
🏓 Phase 3: Table Tennis Championship (April 17–19)
April 17: Round 1 & Practice
  • Format: Round-robin group stage with 4 players per group.
  • Rules: Best of 5 games, each game to 11 points, win by 2.
  • Matches: Group A: Player 1 vs 2 (9:00 AM), 3 vs 4 (10:00 AM); Group B similar (11:00 AM onwards).
  • Practice Sessions: 2:00 PM - Warm-up and skill drills.
  • Equipment: Standard table tennis balls and paddles provided.
April 18: Round 2 & Review
  • Matches: Cross-group matches and second round games.
  • Performance Review: 3:00 PM - Coaches analyze matches and provide feedback.
  • Scoring: Points awarded based on wins; tiebreakers for equal points.
  • Qualification: Top 2 from each group advance.
April 19: Quarterfinals & Preparation
  • Quarterfinals: Knockout matches between group winners and runners-up.
  • Schedule: Match 1 (9:00 AM), Match 2 (10:30 AM), etc.
  • Final Team Prep: 2:00 PM - Strategy sessions for upcoming phases.
  • Explanation: Focus on precision and speed in this fast-paced sport.
🏸 Phase 4: Badminton Championship (April 25–May 2)
April 25-27: League Stage (Group Matches)
  • Format: Round-robin tournament where each team plays every other team in their group.
  • Rules: Best of 3 sets, each set to 21 points. Deuce at 20-20, win by 2 points.
  • Matches: Group A: Team 1 vs Team 2, Team 3 vs Team 4; Group B: Team 5 vs Team 6, Team 7 vs Team 8.
  • Scoring: Win = 2 points, Draw = 1 point, Loss = 0 points.
  • Qualification: Top 2 teams from each group advance to knockout rounds.
April 28-30: Knockout Rounds
  • April 28: Quarterfinals - Winners of Group A vs Runners-up of Group B, etc.
  • April 29: Semifinals - Winners of Quarterfinals.
  • April 30: Third Place Match and Final.
  • Explanation: Single-elimination tournament with same rules as league stage.
🏏 Phase 5: Cricket Championship (May 3–8)
May 3-5: League Stage (Round Robin)
  • Format: T20 cricket matches, each team plays every other team once.
  • Rules: 20 overs per side, standard cricket laws apply. Super Over in case of tie.
  • Matches: Daily fixtures with 2-3 matches per day.
  • Scoring: Win = 2 points, Tie/No Result = 1 point, Loss = 0 points.
  • Qualification: Top 4 teams advance to semifinals based on points, NRR.
May 6-7: Semifinals
  • May 6: Semifinal 1 - 1st vs 4th placed team.
  • May 7: Semifinal 2 - 2nd vs 3rd placed team.
  • Explanation: Winners advance to final in the final week.
🏐 Phase 6: Volleyball Championship (May 9–10)
May 9: League Stage (Group Play)
  • Format: Teams divided into groups, round-robin within groups.
  • Rules: Best of 3 sets, each set to 25 points (win by 2). Rally scoring.
  • Matches: Group A and B matches throughout the day.
  • Scoring: Win = 3 points, Loss = 0 points (unless 3-0 or 3-1).
  • Qualification: Top teams from each group advance to semifinals.
May 10: Knockout Stage
  • Morning: Semifinals.
  • Afternoon: Third Place Match and Final.
  • Explanation: Same rules as league stage, single-elimination.
⏳ Preparation Phase (April 20 – May 10)
  • Daily Practice Sessions (All Teams)
  • Medical & Fitness Checkups
  • Venue Setup & Logistics
  • Media & Sponsorship Coordination
  • Final Match Draw Announcement
🏆 FINAL WEEK - CHAMPIONSHIP FINALS (May 11–15)
May 11: Quarterfinals Round
  • 🏸 Badminton Quarterfinals (Morning): Top 8 players compete in singles and doubles categories. Best of 3 matches, each match best of 3 games (21 points).
  • 🏐 Volleyball Quarterfinals (Afternoon): Top 4 teams enter quarterfinals. Best of 3 sets, 25 points per set. Winners directly advance to semifinals (May 12).
  • 🏏 Cricket Quarterfinal 1 & 2 (Evening): First two quarterfinal matches played. Each match 20 overs per side. Highest-ranked team vs lowest-ranked in QF1; 2nd vs 7th in QF2. Winners advance to semifinals.
  • Timing: 09:00-12:00 (Badminton), 14:00-17:30 (Volleyball), 18:00-21:30 (Cricket)
May 12: Semifinal Marathon
  • 🏸 Badminton Semifinals: 4 remaining players/pairs compete. Winners secure medals and qualify for final. Format: Best of 3 games (21 points each).
  • 🏐 Volleyball Semifinals: Top 2 teams from QF compete. Semifinals are at 2:00 PM and 4:30 PM slots. Winners play final on May 13 (Gold medal match).
  • 🏏 Cricket Quarterfinal 3 & 4: Remaining two quarterfinal matches. Same 20-overs format. Winners join top 2 seeds already in semifinals scheduled for May 13-14.
  • Key Details: All semifinal winners get guaranteed medals. Losers compete for bronze medal match.
May 13: Finals Extravaganza - Day 1
  • 🏸 Badminton Final: Championship decider at 10:00 AM. Best of 3 games for Gold medal. Loser receives Silver medal. Bronze matches held simultaneously on different courts.
  • Bronze Medal Matches (Badminton): 3rd vs 4th place play-off for Bronze at 11:30 AM.
  • 🏐 Volleyball Final: Grand championship match at 3:00 PM. Best of 3 sets. High-energy match with full commentary and live streaming. Loser awarded Silver medal.
  • 🏏 Cricket Semifinal 1: First cricket semifinal at 6:00 PM. 50 overs per side. Winner advances to Grand Final (May 14).
  • Medal Presentation: Badminton and Volleyball medal ceremonies with National Anthem performance at 4:30 PM.
May 14: Finals Extravaganza - Day 2
  • 🏏 Cricket Semifinal 2: Second cricket semifinal at 10:00 AM. 50 overs per side. Winner qualifies for Grand Final in evening. Loser gets second chance in Bronze Medal Match at 3:00 PM (50 overs).
  • 🏆 Cricket Grand Final (Evening Prime Slot - 6:30 PM): Championship match of the tournament! Best teams compete for ultimate glory. 50 overs per side with 15-minute break after powerplay. Full media coverage, commentary in multiple languages, live streaming on all platforms.
  • Match Highlights: Top batsmen, aggressive bowling, strategic timeouts. Winner crowned Champion of L and T Infotech Inter DC Championship 2026!
  • Duration: Approx 4-5 hours including pre-match ceremonies and trophy lifting.
May 15: Grand Celebrations & Awards
  • 🏆 Award Distribution Ceremony (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM): All medal winners (Gold, Silver, Bronze) from all 6 sports felicitated. Chief Guest presents trophies. Each winner receives certificate and prize money - Gold ₹50,000, Silver ₹30,000, Bronze ₹15,000 per sport.
  • 🎤 Closing Speech & Highlights (6:00 PM - 6:45 PM): Director addresses the crowd. Highlights reel of best moments, spectacular catches, match-winning sixes, unbeatable serves shown on giant screen. "Player of the Tournament" awards for standout performers in each sport.
  • 🎉 Celebration & Media Coverage (6:45 PM - 8:00 PM): Photo sessions with champions, media interviews, press conference. Light refreshments and networking. Group photos with Chief Guest, organizing committee, and participants. Virtual coverage on social media with live hashtags.
  • Special Recognition: Most Disciplined Team, Best Sportsman Spirit, Fan Favorite Award, Technical Excellence Award presented.
  • Closing Note: Special performance and announcement for next year's championship. Thank you speech to sponsors, partners, volunteers, and participants.
